Meet Bishop Praise

Born into the family of Mr. and Mrs. Agu in Kaduna, Kaduna State, Nigeria, Vincent Agu who was nicknamed Bishop Praise by virtue of his disposition to praising God is an ardent Psalmist and minister of the gospel domiciled in Nigeria. Bishop Praise's childhood evolved like that of every normal child until an event happened when he was just two years old that changed the story of his life. He knew what it was like to attempt to stand unsupported as a child and then stagger around in the bid to walk until he was able to walk and run around the house all by himself. However, this newly found fun of locomotion was cut short when he caught measles at the age of two which had some complications that crippled his legs. Bishop Praise therefore grew up as a physically challenged person in a suburb of Kaduna that can be described as a ghetto. This made him doubly challenged since he had to live his life dealing with both physical bodily challenge and environmental challenges. Growing up in Kaduna State of Nigeria where most physically challenged persons take to the streets to beg for alms, one could easily imagine that the same fate would be fall Bishop Praise. But God had a different plan for him. Bishop Praise got born again early in life as a teenager and got a new perspective on life. As he grew in the knowledge of God, he grew above the limitations that physical and environmental challenges had set on his path and found his purpose for living. In 1993, Bishop Praise found his life's purpose in the light of Psalm 61:8 which say "So will I sing praises unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my vows" and committed him to it. His commitment and consistency to singing praises unto the Lord as a Psalmist and a minister of the gospel was what got him the name Bishop Praise that has become a household name in the body of Christ in Nigeria. In spite of his physical challenge, by virtue of his availability to serving God's purpose, Bishop Praise has been used of God to be a blessing to His church and the body of Christ tremendously. His ministrations usually bring about an open heaven and great outpouring of the Holy Spirit that compels God's people to draw closer to God. By God's grace and mercy, Bishop Praise had a successful academic pursuit in the field of Accountancy and had contemplated practicing a couple of times, but the demands of ministry would not let him do that. He is a loving husband and a father of two children.
